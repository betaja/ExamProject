OVER FLØJSTRUP BEACH

56.063805, 10.258644
Over Fløjstrup seashore, situated in Denmark, is not only a coastal gem but also a celestial sanctuary. As the waves gently lap against the shore, the night sky above Over Fløjstrup reveals its breathtaking beauty. With minimal light pollution and the soothing sounds of the sea, this coastal haven becomes an ideal spot for night sky enthusiasts. The expansive horizon and clear skies provide remarkable opportunities for both stargazing and astrophotography, offering a tranquil escape where the wonders of the universe meet the rhythmic melody of the ocean at Over Fløjstrup seashore.
